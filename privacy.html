Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

ClerkNova ("ClerkNova", "we", "us") provides AI-powered sales and customer-support software to online stores ("Merchants"). This policy explains what data we handle, why, and the choices available to Merchants and their customers ("End Users").

1. Who is responsible for your data

When you shop or chat on a store that uses ClerkNova, the Merchant is the controller of your personal data and ClerkNova acts as their processor, handling data on the Merchant's behalf and instructions. For ClerkNova's own website and accounts, ClerkNova is the controller.

2. Information we collect

  • Conversation content — messages exchanged with the AI assistant across website chat, WhatsApp, and voice calls, so we can respond and maintain context.
  • Contact details you provide — such as name, email address, or phone number that you share in a conversation for order updates or follow-up.
  • Order and store data — product, order, and fulfillment information retrieved from the Merchant's store (e.g. Shopify) to answer questions and process purchases, returns, or replacements.
  • Technical data — a randomly generated chat session identifier and basic device information needed to operate the chat widget.

3. How we use information

  • To answer questions, recommend products, and complete purchases, returns, and support requests.
  • To maintain conversation history so an ongoing chat keeps its context.
  • To route requests to the Merchant's human team when escalation is needed.
  • With your consent, to send order updates or offers on the channel you provided (e.g. WhatsApp or email).
  • To operate, secure, and improve the service.

4. WhatsApp and Meta

Where a Merchant enables WhatsApp, messages are delivered through the WhatsApp Business Platform and Meta's APIs, subject to Meta's own terms and privacy policies. ClerkNova accesses only the WhatsApp Business assets a Merchant explicitly connects, and uses them solely to operate messaging and product-catalog features for that Merchant.

5. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with: the Merchant whose store you interacted with; infrastructure and AI processing providers strictly to run the service (e.g. cloud hosting, language-model and speech providers, messaging platforms); and where required by law. Providers are bound to use the data only to provide their services to us.

6. Retention

Conversation and contact data are retained for as long as needed to provide the service to the Merchant, and are deleted or anonymized on the Merchant's instruction or when no longer required. Merchants can request deletion of their store's data by contacting us.

7. Your choices & rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data, and to withdraw consent to marketing messages at any time. To exercise these rights for data held on a Merchant's behalf, contact the Merchant or email us and we will assist them in responding.

8. Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and restricted networking to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information using appropriate safeguards.

9. Children

ClerkNova is not directed to children and we do not knowingly collect data from children under the age required by applicable law.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

11. Contact

Questions or requests: support@clerknova.com.
