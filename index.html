What it does

Everything a great store employee does

Not a chatbot with canned answers — an AI agent that takes real actions on your store, in your customers' own words.

Sells on your storefront

Understands what shoppers need, recommends the right products with swipeable cards, and creates secure checkout links — the whole sale happens in chat.

Runs your WhatsApp shop
