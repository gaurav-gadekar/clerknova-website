Install & connect

Add ClerkNova from the Shopify admin and toggle the chat widget on in your theme. Products and policies sync automatically.

Connect WhatsApp

One click, log in with Facebook, pick your business number — catalog, webhooks and tokens configure themselves.

Set your rules

Return window, support phone, escalation preferences. The AI follows your policy — and hands off to humans when you want it to.
